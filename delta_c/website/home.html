What is DeltaC?

We aim to create interactive web-based materials for high-school classes on statistical issues related to climate and climate-change, specifically targeting material for Advanced Placement statistics classes. We will be leveraging new web technologies to allow students to easily assemble, analyze, and model data to detect trends in and make connections between the atmosphere, the ocean, ecosystems, and human society. DeltaC is sponsored by the STATMOS network at the University of Washington, with funding from the National Science Foundation.
